The Best Movies According to Bhavana

My top 3 movies of all time

Lucky Bhaaskar

I love this movie as it explains the peccable journey of a poor man making his way into the rich life.

Watch Lucky Bhaaskar on Netflix

Jathiratnalu

This movie plot is not excellent, but the comedy track in this movie has made the audience laugh their lungs out. It's a family movie and a must watch

Watch Jathiratnalu on prime

Maharaja

This Tamizh movie starring Vijay Sethupathi has one of the best twists. This is about a man who keeps committing endless crimes or is a standby to crimes. It shows that evil makes its way back to you, that's how fate is.

Watch Maharaja on Netflix
